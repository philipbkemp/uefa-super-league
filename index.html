UEFA Super League

The floated idea in the real world to have a "Super League" amongst the biggest teams in Europe rekindled an idea I had many years ago - what if there was one league that covered every team in Europe. Not just a select few. But every team from every country that UEFA recognises. I had some spare time, some website building skills, and an empty GitHub Project, so this is that league.

First of, the rules have their own page, as my head isn't the best place to store this. Basically, a win is always worth 3 points, teams are ranked based on their Points Per Game score, the Super League is split into 8 Divisions, each with 100 teams, and at the end of each season, 10 teams (normally) get promoted/relegated between each Division. There are also some iconography conventions to be aware of.

There is also a list of winning clubs and winning nations.
